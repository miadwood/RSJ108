How to become TikTok famous

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how so many people have viral videos on the for you page? It's actually really simple! Follow the steps and you'll have a viral video and followers in no time!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Smart phone
  • The app TikTok
  • Good lighting
  • A trendy look
  • A trendy or original concept
  • Good hashtags

  1. While starting to film, set up or hold your phone and face the lighting.

    This is necessary for the audience to see you clearly. When a TikTok has good quality due to lighting, it more likely to end up on the for you page.

  2. Choose a trendy audio, but if you prefer to make your own, go ahead!

    Remember, TikTok is all audio and visual based, so if there is a good audio with the video setting, you are almost sure to go viral.

    April 2021 Trending TikTok Sounds

  3. When done filming, you can add text to your video for the audience to read as they watch.

    This has become really popular. You can add closed captioning to videos of you or others talking, or add text for any reason you like. You can even change the duration for how long you want the text on the screen.

  4. Add filters and effects to make the video stand out more.

    Everyone loves to see cool videos with effects. It makes them less boring to watch and more likely to go viral.

  5. Add your caption, hashtags, and hit post!

    You can always find a clever caption on a viral post. Use hashtags to appeal to the audience you think would like your video. Just hit post and hope for the best.
